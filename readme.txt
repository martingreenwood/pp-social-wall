This plugin enables you to use a social wall on your website.

== Description ==

This plugin displays a wall of social posts from facebook, twitter and instagram on your website.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `pp-social-wall` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. navigate to Settings > WP Social Wall and enter your facebook, twitter and instagram settings.
4. Acvtivate your licence to receive updates

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
